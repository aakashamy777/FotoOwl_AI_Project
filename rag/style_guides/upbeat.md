# Style: Upbeat

## Pacing
Fast cuts. 1.5-2.5 seconds per scene. High energy rhythm.

## Visual Treatment
Bright, saturated colors. High contrast. Occasional quick zoom-punch on beat.

## Caption Tone
Bold, energetic captions. Exclamations okay. Sans-serif, heavy weight fonts.
Pop-in or bounce animation on text entry.

## Transitions
Hard cuts, whip pans, quick slide transitions. Match cuts to implied beat.

## Music/Emotion Cue
Fun, celebratory, high energy. Suited for birthdays, parties, celebrations.
