# Style: Corporate

## Pacing
Steady, even. 3-4 seconds per scene. Predictable rhythm, no surprises.

## Visual Treatment
Clean, neutral color grade. Minimal saturation boost. No vignette or grain.

## Caption Tone
Professional, informative captions. Full short sentences allowed. Clean sans-serif font.
Simple fade or slide-up entry, no bounce or playful motion.

## Transitions
Subtle crossfade or clean slide. No whip pans, no flashy effects.

## Music/Emotion Cue
Professional, confident, understated. Suited for corporate highlights, product events, conferences.
