# Style: Cinematic

## Pacing
Slow, deliberate. 4-6 seconds per scene minimum. Let moments breathe.

## Visual Treatment
Warm color grade, subtle vignette, letterbox bars (2.35:1 crop feel) optional.
Ken Burns slow zoom/pan on stills rather than static frames.

## Caption Tone
Minimal text. Short, evocative phrases (3-6 words). Serif or elegant sans font.
Fade in/out, never hard cuts on text.

## Transitions
Crossfade or slow dissolve between scenes. Avoid hard cuts, wipes, or flashy effects.

## Music/Emotion Cue
Emotional, romantic, nostalgic feel. Suited for weddings, anniversaries, tributes.
